## What is DuckTape WMS?

DuckTape WMS is a state-of-the-art warehouse management system that, like its namesake, sticks all your operations together in one place. It's built for businesses that need to keep their ducks in a row!

<Note>
  Unlike regular duct tape, our system won't leave a sticky residue on your warehouse operations!
</Note>

## Why DuckTape?

* **Quack Response Times** - Lightning-fast inventory updates
* **No Fowl Play** - Robust security for all your data
* **Bills That Won't Break The Bank** - Affordable pricing for businesses of all sizes
* **Duck-umentation** - You're reading it right now!
